Safe jet washing and graffiti removal

A practical guide to graffiti removal, careful jet washing and when to ask for help with marked exterior surfaces.

Get a free quoteCall 07920 401090

Graffiti, paint marks and stubborn stains need a careful approach. The goal is to clean the surface without damaging brickwork, paving, render or nearby areas.

Most customers do not need a complicated survey to get started. Clear photos, the town or postcode, and a short note about the problem are usually enough for practical advice.

What to check first

  • Take photos of the marked surface from a distance and close up.
  • Mention what the surface is: brick, stone, concrete, render or paving.
  • Do not keep scrubbing with harsh chemicals if the surface is delicate.
  • Ask for advice before using very high pressure on old brickwork or joints.

What to do next

If you are in Guildford, Godalming, Farnham, Haslemere, Surrey, West Sussex or nearby Hampshire, send photos and ask for the most sensible next step.

Need help with this?

Send the photos through the quote form and include your phone number, postcode and the surface you want cleaned.
